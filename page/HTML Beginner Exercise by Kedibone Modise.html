What is 4C hair?

4C hair is a hair type that falls under curly hair. The curls or coils form the letter "C" and it is well known for its shrinkage as the hair appears shorter when dry compared to its actual length when stretched.

Here are the general characteristics of 4C hair:

  • Coarse strand texture with extremely tight kinks
  • Very little kink/coil definition
  • Full of natural volume and lift
  • Most fragile hair type and prone to breakage

The five essential steps of any 4c natural hair wash day routine are:

  1. Pre-shampoo care: pre-poo & detangle
  2. Wash hair: shampoo & condition
  3. Deep condition: mask, steam & rinse
  4. Prepare hair for styling: dry hair & infuse it with additional moisture
  5. Style hair

4C hair also has the most shrinkage of all curly hair types, which means that it can appear much shorter than it is. Another unique feature of 4C hair is its versatility. 4C hair can be twisted, braided, or styled in an afro without chemicals or heat.
